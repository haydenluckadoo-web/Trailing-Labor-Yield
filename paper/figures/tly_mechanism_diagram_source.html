Trailing Labor Yield (TLY)

The historical pay pool grows as a sum of parts. On exit, the trailing payout starts from the final active bonus only, not from the full accumulated historical pool.

Former contributors pull claims from the treasury. The amount tapers over a fixed duration rather than compounding forever.
In the contracts, missed legacy epochs can be claimed in bounded batches, but the payout basis still comes from the final active bonus alone.

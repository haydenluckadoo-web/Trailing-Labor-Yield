Trailing Labor Yield (TLY)

A contributor earns normal active compensation, exits into a snapshot based on the final active bonus, and then receives a tapered trailing payout over time.

1. While Active
Base Pay + Active Bonus

The contributor receives normal pay each epoch and also earns an active bonus while the historical pay pool keeps building.

Active bonus = accrual share × (historical pool + base pay)
→
2. On Exit
Snapshot the Final Active Bonus

When the contributor leaves, the protocol locks in the last active bonus as the starting amount for the trailing claim.

Initial trailing amount = final active bonus
→
3. Legacy State
Tapered Trailing Payout

The former contributor claims a payout each epoch, and that payout tapers over a fixed duration instead of compounding forever.

Later payout = snapshot amount × taper factor over elapsed time
Only the final active bonus is snapshotted at exit. The full historical pay pool is not directly paid out.
